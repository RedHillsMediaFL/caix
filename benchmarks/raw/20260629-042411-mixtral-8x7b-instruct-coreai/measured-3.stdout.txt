The future of local inferencing is likely to be shaped by ongoing advancements in data analysis techniques, computational power, and the increasing availability of granular data. Local inferencing refers to the process of making inferences or predictions about specific subgroups or locations, as opposed to global inferences that apply to entire populations or regions.


The future of local inference, particularly in the context of artificial intelligence and machine learning, is a topic of significant interest and ongoing development. Local inference refers to the process of performing inference tasks (such as prediction, classification, or decision-making) directly on a local device, rather than relying on a centralized server or cloud infrastructure. This approach offers several advantages, including enhanced privacy, reduced latency, and increased efficiency.
